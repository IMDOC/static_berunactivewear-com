Tell Us About Your Custom Activewear Project — Attach Your Tech-Pack or Reference, We Send Back Feasibility, Sampling Route, and an FOB Estimate

Attach whatever exists — tech-pack, product photos, or a link to the piece you want to beat — plus your quantity and deadline. Anna comes back within 24 hours with feasibility, a sampling route, and the questions that move your project forward.

BEFORE YOU SEND

A better first message gets a more useful quotation.

If you already have a tech-pack, attach it. If not, send product photos, target fabric, quantity range, logo method, label/packing needs and destination. The reply can then focus on feasibility, sampling route and the evidence files your team needs.

  • For OEM: tech-pack, fabric reference, size curve, trims and packing rules.
  • For ODM: target product family, market position, preferred fabric hand feel and logo placement.
  • For compliance: request current PDFs, audit summaries, QC templates or packing examples during RFQ.
  • For team orders: player roster (CSV or spreadsheet), crest artwork in vector format, and the date the kit must be in hand.
  • File formats: PDF or AI for tech-packs and artwork; physical reference garments can be couriered — ask for the address.

COME SEE IT

Visiting our Guangzhou floor — advance notice appreciated; NDA available.

Give us three to five days’ notice and we will arrange the visit end to end: an invitation letter for your visa if needed, pickup from your Guangzhou hotel or the airport, and a translator if your technical team prefers one.
