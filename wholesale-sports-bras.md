## 板块 5: Dual-Axis Band×Cup Grading [band×cup size-grid + proportional-regrading callout]
**H2**: Real Band×Cup Grading for Wholesale Sports Bra Sizing
**Sub**: A 32A and a 38DD are not a small and a large of the same block. We grade band and cup as two independent axes, so your large cups fit like the sample instead of a stretched medium.
**内容**:
- **Band×cup size-grid**: band 30–42 (rows) × cup A–DD (columns) — a true bra size matrix, confirmed on your approved sample before bulk.
- **Two-axis grading method**: band circumference graded on one axis (underband length, wing depth, closure rows); cup volume graded on a separate axis (cradle width, cup depth, apex position) — proportional cradle / underband / wing / strap-anchor regrading per cup size, not a single linear S–XL rule.
- **Large-cup treatment**: D and DD cups get wider wings, power-mesh side panels, wider cushioned straps, and a reinforced strap anchor so the band does not ride up and the cup does not spill.
- **Why it matters**: linear-grading a 38DD from a 34B block gives a gaping band, cup spillage, digging straps, and a rolling underband — the failure that concentrates returns in exactly the sizes a size-inclusive or intimates-positioned brand promises. Two-axis grading holds the fit your sample showed across the full 30–42 / A–DD range.
