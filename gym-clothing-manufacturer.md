**Q1: Can you really handle staff + trainer + retail + class apparel in one PO?**
A: Yes. Our 12 production lines accommodate woven (front-desk polos), heavy-jersey (trainer wear), lifestyle knits (member retail), and performance fabric (class-specific) on parallel lines. One project manager owns the entire program. One Pantone reference. One label spec. One consolidated shipment.

**Q2: What's the MOQ if I split across 4 roles?**
A: MOQ is 300/SKU per role, but the 300 splits across XS-XXL (not multiplied per size). A realistic chain PO is roughly: 60 front-desk polos · 200 trainer tees · 300 retail hoodies · 80 class-specific tanks = 4 SKUs, 640 total pieces. We can also accept lower per-role MOQ (60-100/SKU) via Private Label combined with Gym Retail Pack.

**Q3: How do you keep my logo identical across reorders and across the 4 roles?**
A: Per-PO logo dimension QC at ±2mm tolerance, ΔE ≤1.5 vs your reference Pantone (published commitment). Single embroidery/print supplier across all 4 roles. Cross-PO sample retained and matched against new PO at first-piece inspection.

**Q4: Can I order the gym retail pack as a sample bundle before committing?**
A: Yes. We send 4 sample SKUs (one per role) at USD 45 each (USD 180 total, waived after first PO). Sample-pack lead time 7-10 working days. You can pull photography from the sample pack before scheduling your bulk PO.

### Group B: Brand-side gym apparel sourcing (ICP-A)

**Q5: What's your abrasion-test threshold for trainer-grade gym wear?**
A: Martindale ISO 12947-2 at 30,000+ cycles on all gym-tier fabric. Industry baseline for general activewear is 15-20K. We test every fabric lot before cut. Test report included in pre-shipment QC package on request.

**Q6: How do you handle sweat-pH dye degradation on dark colors?**
A: ISO 105-E04 acid sweat (pH 5.5) + alkaline sweat (pH 8.0) colorfastness at grade 4+ wet rub and dry rub. Dye system selected for oxidative stability against deodorant aluminum salts. Underarm yellowing on whites tested via AATCC 15 perspiration colorfastness.

**Q7: Which fabric mills do you source from for gym apparel?**
A: Eclat Textile (4-way stretch specialist), Formosa Taffeta (recycled-poly performance), Brookwood Performance (heavy-jersey knit specialist). All three OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified at mill level. Higg-traceable on request.

**Q8: Can you produce GRS-certified recycled polyester gym apparel for eco lines?**
A: Yes. GRS certification on file (CU 1014387 GRS-2024). Recycled polyester gym tees, joggers, and hoodies available with full chain-of-custody documentation. Used by 2 of our current customers for their eco capsules.

### Group C: First-time gym sourcing (ICP-C)

**Q9: I don't have a tech-pack. Can you still help with gym wear?**
A: Yes — go with ODM. We have 50+ gym-cut block patterns (training tees, joggers, 5"/7"/9" inseam shorts, hoodies, compression). You customize color, print, fabric tier, and labels. No tech-pack required.

**Q10: Can I order just 1 sample to test before committing to 300?**
A: Yes. 1-piece sample, USD 45 fee, produced in 5 working days when fabric is on stock. Sample fee waived after first bulk PO. You can order samples in all 6 gym subcategories before deciding which to scale.
