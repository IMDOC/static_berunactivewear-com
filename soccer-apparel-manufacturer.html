Adult amateur 11-a-side. Youth U10-U18. College. Corporate Sunday. 7-a-side and Futsal. Charity tournament. Each tier has its own SKU mix, sponsor rule, and sizing curve.

Adult Amateur 11-a-side

SKU Mix
Home + away + third match jersey, match shorts, knee-high socks, GK jersey + GK shorts, training shirt + shorts, training bib
Sponsor Rule
3-5 sponsor placements typical: main chest + secondary sleeve + league patch + crest; brand-standards file compliance critical
Sizing & Fabric
Adult M-XXL; 18-25 player roster; 140-160 GSM match + 180 GSM training

Youth U10-U18

SKU Mix
The full six-SKU kit graded into youth curves — YS-YM at U10, YM-YL at U12, YL through adult M-L from U14 up

College / University Intramural

Color-clash compliance

Referee rules (FIFA Laws of the Game plus most national federation rule books) require the goalkeeper's jersey to be a different color from both team's outfield jerseys AND from the referee's jersey. We auto-check your GK kit color against your outfield kit AND the league's referee jersey color before bulk runs. Out-of-spec GK colors get an alternate suggestion within 24 hours.

Sponsor brand-standards ingestion

Submit your sponsor's brand-standards PDF or vector logo file per sponsor at order entry. PM reviews against your tech-pack within 48 hours, flags clear-space / sizing / panel-seam exclusion rules, and ships a sponsor-comp pre-bulk sample for brand-standards sign-off before bulk PO runs.

12-month panel retention + sponsor-layer re-print

Your sublimation panel + spec file + each sponsor logo file is retained on our server for 12 months. Re-orders skip panel rebuild. Mid-season sponsor changes get the 14-day re-batch turn because the sponsor layer is independent from the base design. For multi-sport team uniforms at family level, see /products/team-club.

Soccer Kit Failures Run on a Calendar: Three We Refuse to Repeat

A soccer season does not wait for a factory. Each case below came from a buyer whose previous vendor missed the only date that mattered — kickoff, a sponsor compliance deadline, or the week a replacement jersey was needed on the pitch.

1 Mid-Season Replacement Treated as a Brand-New Order

Pain

Three players grew, lost, or shredded jerseys eight weeks in. The factory’s answer: order another hundred.

Youth club director, $14,000 pre-season PO across 6 U10-U14 teams. Eight weeks in: one U12 keeper grew 4cm, one U10 lost his away jersey, one U14 shredded his on turf. Factory terms for 4 replacement pieces: another full run, six more weeks — half the remaining season. Parents complained. Sponsor brand-standards photographer flagged the mismatched kid in the team photo.

Our fix

A 14-day replacement lane that lives inside the season calendar

Up to a fifth of the original PO can be called back at any point in the 12-month retention window, because the sublimation panel, roster file, and sponsor art never leave our server. Replacements enter that morning’s scheduling board as ship-date-priority work — not as a new order at the back of the queue — which is what makes a 14-day turn honest. Per-size specification (1 U12 / 2 U10 / 1 U14), one PM from the original order through every replacement, and multi-team programs roll their replacement needs into one quarterly PO.

2 Per-Player Registration Drift Costs a Sponsorship

Pain

22 jerseys shipped. 3 had numbers off, 1 had a name typo, 5 had sponsor logos crossing the panel seam.

Amateur 11-a-side captain. Match-day inspection found 17 of 22 jerseys correct. On 3 jerseys the back NUMBER drifted 3mm off-center vs the NAME — visible from behind. On 1 jersey the NAME was typo’d. On 5 jerseys the chest sponsor logo crossed the panel seam, violating brewery sponsor brand-standards Clause 4.2. Threat: pull next-season's $2,500 sponsorship.

Our fix

±2mm 5-element registration, overhead-camera template, name-entry double-key

All five placement elements — number, name, main sponsor, secondary sponsor, league patch, crest — carry a ±2mm tolerance, checked against the PO template as each jersey passes the press. The roster CSV is keyed twice by different operators, so a typo has to happen twice in the same cell to survive. Sponsor brand-standards files are read at order entry, panel-seam exclusion zones get flagged before bulk, and a sponsor-comp sample ships for sign-off first.

3 Sponsor Rebrand Mid-Season, Every Factory Says 6 Weeks

Pain

Main sponsor rebranded mid-season. 264 jerseys needed in 14 days. Four factories all said six weeks.

Regional league organizer, 12 teams under one brewery sponsor. Mid-season: brewery announces a logo rebrand with a 30-day compliance deadline or lose $18,000 in payouts. 264 jerseys need re-batch (12 teams × 22 jerseys), preserving team design + player name + number + crest. Vendor 1: 6 weeks + $5,800 setup. Vendor 2: full re-specification. Vendor 3: couldn’t preserve per-player names. Vendor 4: heat-press that peels in 4-6 washes.

Our fix

14-day re-batch, sponsor-layer independent, 12-month panel retention, multi-team consolidation

14-day mid-season sponsor re-batch turn. Original sublimation panel + spec file + each sponsor logo file retained 12 months on our server — no panel rebuild required. Sponsor layer printed independently from base design; only the sponsor layer updates, base panel + player name + number + crest + secondary sponsors preserved untouched. PM reviews new sponsor brand-standards file within 48 hours. Multi-team programs consolidate ALL teams' re-batches into one PO with one sponsor-comp sample sign-off cycle.

FAQ

Questions Soccer Buyers Ask Us

Eight answers, split between clubs ordering their first kit and coordinators already deep in a season.

Group A — For first-time squad organizers
Where are your order minimums, sample fees, and lead times published?

On the company-wide FAQ — one page kept current for every production track, covering MOQ mechanics, sampling, and how the Soccer Squad Pack distributes its floor across a six-SKU kit.

Our season opens in six weeks. Is that survivable?

Yes — if the roster locks this week. The team track runs 18-25 days from roster lock to ship; add transit and you still clear a six-week kickoff with margin. What actually kills six-week windows is roster churn after panel layout, so we freeze the CSV at sample sign-off and route late additions through the replacement lane instead of reopening the bulk run.

Do you produce goalkeeper jerseys with referee color-clash compliance?

Yes — as a dedicated goalkeeper jersey manufacturer, color-clash compliance is mandatory on every GK kit we produce. We auto-check your GK kit color against both your outfield kits AND your league's referee jersey color (sourced from your league's rule book) before bulk runs. If a clash is detected, we recommend an alternate GK color before your next business day closes. GK jerseys ship with long-sleeve construction + elbow pad inserts + reflective shoulder accent as standard.

Can you ingest a sponsor brand-standards file and verify compliance before bulk?

Yes. Submit your sponsor's brand-standards PDF or vector logo file per sponsor at order entry. PM reviews against your tech-pack within 48 hours, flags clear-space / sizing / panel-seam exclusion rules, and ships a sponsor-comp pre-bulk sample (1 complete jersey with ALL sponsors + player name + number + crest + league patch on grid background with measurement tape overlay) for brand-standards sign-off before bulk PO runs.

Group B — For returning customers, multi-team coordinators, and mid-season urgency
How does the squad-flex re-order rule work for replacement jerseys mid-season?

Up to a fifth of the original volume can come back through the replacement lane inside 12 months, in any size mix (say 1 U12 / 2 U10 / 1 U14), and it ships in 14 days because the panel and sponsor art are still sitting on our server. There is no new quantity floor — the lane exists precisely for the 3-piece and 5-piece emergencies a season produces.

My main sponsor rebranded mid-season. How long does a mid-season sponsor re-batch take?

14 calendar days for sponsor-logo-only update on the original PO panel — base design + player name + number + crest + secondary sponsors stay untouched. We retain the original sublimation panel + per-sponsor logo file for 12 months, and our sublimation line is configured so the sponsor layer is independently re-printable without re-running the base print. Send us your new sponsor brand-standards file on day 1; we produce a sponsor-comp sample on day 5-7 for sign-off, then bulk re-print on day 9-12, ship on day 13-14.

What’s your per-player number, name, and sponsor registration tolerance?

±2mm on all 5 placement elements (player number + player name + main sponsor + secondary sponsor + league patch + crest), verified jersey-by-jersey under the overhead camera at the press. Anything outside tolerance is reprinted before it can be packed, and names are keyed twice from your CSV by separate operators — a one-keystroke typo cannot reach a finished jersey.

I’m coordinating 12 teams for a regional league. Can you consolidate all 12 squad orders into one PO with per-team sponsor and crest preservation?

Yes — consolidating many teams under one order is exactly what the Soccer Squad Pack is for: a single PM, PO number, and invoice covering the whole league. Each team rides in the master order as its own spec layer — sponsor, crest, and roster intact — and if the league’s shared sponsor changes mid-season, every affected team re-batches under one PO with a single comp-sample approval. Delivery typically structured per league calendar (Apertura kit ships before season opener, Clausura kit ships before mid-season break).
