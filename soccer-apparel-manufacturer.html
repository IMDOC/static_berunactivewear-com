Color-clash compliance

Referee rules (FIFA Laws of the Game plus most national federation rule books) require the goalkeeper's jersey to be a different color from both team's outfield jerseys AND from the referee's jersey. We auto-check your GK kit color against your outfield kit AND the league's referee jersey color before bulk runs. Out-of-spec GK colors get an alternate suggestion within 24 hours.

Sponsor brand-standards ingestion

Submit your sponsor's brand-standards PDF or vector logo file per sponsor at order entry. PM reviews against your tech-pack within 48 hours, flags clear-space / sizing / panel-seam exclusion rules, and ships a sponsor-comp pre-bulk sample for brand-standards sign-off before bulk PO runs.

12-month panel retention + sponsor-layer re-print

Your sublimation panel + spec file + each sponsor logo file is retained on our server for 12 months. Re-orders skip panel rebuild. Mid-season sponsor changes get the 14-day re-batch turn because the sponsor layer is independent from the base design. For multi-sport team uniforms at family level, see /products/team-club.

Our fix

Squad-flex 20% re-order, zero added MOQ, 14-day turn, 12-month panel retention

Single PM continuity from original PO. Multi-team programs consolidate all teams' mid-season re-orders into one quarterly re-order PO.

2 Per-Player Registration Drift Costs a Sponsorship

Pain

22 jerseys shipped. 3 had numbers off, 1 had a name typo, 5 had sponsor logos crossing the panel seam.

Amateur 11-a-side captain. Match-day inspection found 17 of 22 jerseys correct. On 3 jerseys the back NUMBER drifted 3mm off-center vs the NAME — visible from behind. On 1 jersey the NAME was typo’d. On 5 jerseys the chest sponsor logo crossed the panel seam, violating brewery sponsor brand-standards Clause 4.2. Threat: pull next-season's $2,500 sponsorship.

Our fix

±2mm 5-element registration, overhead-camera template, name-entry double-key

±2mm tolerance on all 5 placement elements (number, name, main sponsor, secondary sponsor, league patch, crest) — overhead-camera registration template QC per jersey. Name-entry double-key verification: two operators enter each name independently from your roster CSV; system flags discrepancies before printing. Sponsor brand-standards file ingested at order entry, panel-seam exclusion rules flagged pre-bulk. Sponsor-comp pre-bulk sample shipped for brand-standards sign-off.

3 Sponsor Rebrand Mid-Season, Every Factory Says 6 Weeks

Main sponsor rebranded mid-season. 264 jerseys needed in 14 days. Four factories all said six weeks.

Regional league organizer, 12 teams under one brewery sponsor. Mid-season: brewery announces a logo rebrand with a 30-day compliance deadline or lose $18,000 in payouts. 264 jerseys need re-batch (12 teams × 22 jerseys), preserving team design + player name + number + crest. Vendor 1: 6 weeks + $5,800 setup. Vendor 2: full re-specification. Vendor 3: couldn’t preserve per-player names. Vendor 4: heat-press that peels in 4-6 washes.

14-day mid-season sponsor re-batch turn. Original sublimation panel + spec file + each sponsor logo file retained 12 months on our server — no panel rebuild required. Sponsor layer printed independently from base design; only the sponsor layer updates, base panel + player name + number + crest + secondary sponsors preserved untouched. FAQ

Soccer Apparel Manufacturer FAQ

Eight questions soccer buyers ask before sending a squad brief. Four for first-time squad organizers, four for returning customers and mid-season urgency.

Group A — For first-time squad organizers
What’s the MOQ for a single soccer SKU like a match jersey or GK kit, and how does the Soccer Squad Pack MOQ split work?

Single-SKU MOQ is 300 pieces baseline (split across XS-XXL from that 300, not multiplied per size). 200/SKU on ODM block patterns. 100/style on Private Label. For a full multi-SKU squad kit, ask about the Soccer Squad Pack rule — 220 cross-SKU one PO with jersey + shorts ≥80 each as the mandatory floor and other SKUs (socks / training shirt / GK / training bib) flexible 30-50 each.

Can I order 1 sample to test before committing to a 300-piece bulk?

Yes. 1-piece sample, USD 45 fee, 5 working days when fabric is in stock. Sample fee waived after first bulk PO. You can sample any of the 6 soccer SKUs before deciding (match jersey, goalkeeper jersey, training shirt, shorts, socks, training bib).

Do you produce goalkeeper jerseys with referee color-clash compliance?

Yes — as a dedicated goalkeeper jersey manufacturer, color-clash compliance is mandatory on every GK kit we produce. We auto-check your GK kit color against both your outfield kits AND your league's referee jersey color (sourced from your league's rule book) before bulk runs. If a clash is detected, we recommend an alternate GK color within 24 hours. GK jerseys ship with long-sleeve construction + elbow pad inserts + reflective shoulder accent as standard.

Can you ingest a sponsor brand-standards file and verify compliance before bulk?

Yes. Submit your sponsor's brand-standards PDF or vector logo file per sponsor at order entry. PM reviews against your tech-pack within 48 hours, flags clear-space / sizing / panel-seam exclusion rules, and ships a sponsor-comp pre-bulk sample (1 complete jersey with ALL sponsors + player name + number + crest + league patch on grid background with measurement tape overlay) for brand-standards sign-off before bulk PO runs.

Group B — For returning customers, multi-team coordinators, and mid-season urgency
How does the squad-flex re-order rule work for replacement jerseys mid-season?

Squad-flex re-order allows up to 20% of your original PO volume to be re-ordered at zero added MOQ + 14-day turn. Example: your original PO was 100 pieces — you can re-order up to 20 pieces (lost / grown-out / damaged) within 12 months at zero added MOQ. Per-size specification supported (e.g., 1 U12 / 2 U10 / 1 U14). Original sublimation panel + sponsor logo file retained on our server for the 12-month window — no panel rebuild required.

My main sponsor rebranded mid-season. How long does a mid-season sponsor re-batch take?

14 calendar days for sponsor-logo-only update on the original PO panel — base design + player name + number + crest + secondary sponsors stay untouched. We retain the original sublimation panel + per-sponsor logo file for 12 months, and our sublimation line is configured so the sponsor layer is independently re-printable without re-running the base print. Send us your new sponsor brand-standards file on day 1; we produce a sponsor-comp sample on day 5-7 for sign-off, then bulk re-print on day 9-12, ship on day 13-14.

What’s your per-player number, name, and sponsor registration tolerance?

±2mm on all 5 placement elements (player number + player name + main sponsor + secondary sponsor + league patch + crest), verified per jersey by overhead-camera registration template QC at sublimation station. Out-of-tolerance jerseys are pulled off the line and re-printed before packing. Player name entry uses double-key verification (two operators enter independently from your roster CSV; system flags discrepancies before printing) to eliminate single-operator typo failures.

I’m coordinating 12 teams for a regional league. Can you consolidate all 12 squad orders into one PO with per-team sponsor and crest preservation?

Yes — multi-team consolidation is the core of the Soccer Squad Pack. One PM, one PO number, one invoice across all teams. Each team's sponsor + crest + player roster is preserved as a separate spec layer in the master order. Mid-season sponsor re-batches consolidate across all teams into one re-batch PO with one sponsor-comp sample sign-off cycle. Delivery typically structured per league calendar (Apertura kit ships before season opener, Clausura kit ships before mid-season break).
