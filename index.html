⌜⌝ Engagement tracks

OEM, ODM & Private Label Services

Send a finished tech-pack, a rough sketch, or a reference sample, and we take it to bulk.

01

OEM (Cut-and-Sew)

You supply the tech-pack — measurements, seam allowances, fabric spec and artwork — and we cut, sew and finish bulk to it. Your approved sample becomes the line standard; QC checks every production piece against that sample, not just the spec sheet. 100-piece MOQ per SKU. Bulk runs 35–42 days on stock fabric, 48–58 days when fabric is custom-ordered.

02

ODM

You pick a base silhouette from our block library, then specify colour, logo placement, fabric weight or finish. Because the pattern already exists, you skip the USD 220 drafting stage and save 7–14 days. MOQ is 100 pcs per SKU; bulk runs 30–40 days from deposit confirmation.

03

Private Label

An add-on to OEM or ODM, not a separate track. You supply hangtags, woven care labels, polybags and outer-carton marks; we sew them in and pack to your retail floor-ready spec. Cost is quoted per unit alongside the production line; no extra MOQ layer.
