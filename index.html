Materials & Methods

Fabric and Production Technology

The technical capabilities behind every garment we ship.

4-Way Stretch Knit

Nylon/spandex blends with ≥40% elongation for leggings, bras, and compression tops.

Moisture-Wicking Finish

Hydrophilic coating on polyester and blends. Wicks perspiration from skin to fabric surface.

Recycled Polyester (GRS)

GRS-certified rPET yarn for eco-conscious product lines. Chain-of-custody documented.

Sublimation Printing

Full-bleed dye sublimation on polyester. Unlimited colors, zero hand feel, wash-fast.

Compression Grade

Graduated compression panels in 180–280 GSM nylon/spandex for performance and recovery wear.

DWR & Bonded Seam

Durable water repellent coating + bonded/welded seams for outdoor and running shells.
