**H2:** ODM or OEM -- Which Track Fits Your Project?

| Dimension | ODM | OEM |
|-----------|-----|-----|
| Pattern source | Factory library (200+ ready) | Your tech-pack |
| Pattern cost | Zero | USD 220+/pattern |
| Customization depth | Fabric, color, logo, minor fit | Full custom from scratch |
| MOQ | 200/SKU | 300/SKU |
| Sample lead time | 5-7 days | 5-12 days |
| Bulk lead time | 30-40 days | 35-42 days |
| Best for | Market testing, first launch, speed-to-shelf | Proprietary design, exact spec control |

Most brands start with ODM for their first 2-3 SKUs, then move to OEM once they have validated fit and demand with real sales data.
