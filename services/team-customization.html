Roster Workflow

From Spreadsheet to Finished Kit in Four Steps

1

Upload CSV

Fill three columns: player name, number, size. One row per piece. Save as .csv and attach to your inquiry.

2

Data Validation

Pre-press checks for duplicate numbers, character-length overflows, and missing sizes within 24 hours. A marked-up proof sheet is returned if corrections are needed.

3

Per-Piece Sublimation

Each piece runs through the sublimation line with its own merged file — number, name, crest, and sponsor block composited from your validated roster.

4

QC Cross-Check

Every finished piece is scanned against the original roster row: correct name spelling, correct number, correct size label. Mismatches trigger reprint before packing.

Pre-Production Checklist

What We Need Before Production Starts

  • Roster CSV

    .csv format, UTF-8 encoding, columns: name | number | size. A downloadable template is available on the contact page.

  • Design Files

    Team crest in vector (AI/EPS/SVG), sponsor artwork as a separate layer, jersey template in PDF or AI with bleed marks.

  • Size Chart Confirmation

    Finalized size run (XS–3XL distribution) locked before sample approval. Changes after approval reset the 18–25 day production clock.

  • Sample Approval Cutoff

    Written approval required via email before bulk production begins. Unapproved samples hold the queue.
