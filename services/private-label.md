# Step 1 Output - Audience & Pain

## Primary ICP

**ICP-A (Established Sportswear Brand) + ICP-B (Fitness Studio Chain Owner)**

- **ICP-A** lands here after scanning /services and clicking the Private Label card. She already runs OEM or ODM orders and wants to confirm private labeling is a bolt-on to her existing PO -- not a separate production run with its own MOQ. She needs artwork specs (file format, bleed, color mode) so she can brief her design team before inquiry.
- **ICP-B** runs a 12-location gym chain and wants branded training tees for members and coaches. He already chose ODM (existing patterns) on /services. Now he needs to know what label components are included and what artwork files his freelance designer must prepare.

Per site-strategy-pack SS2 ICP convergence rules: `/services/private-label` primary = ICP-A. ICP-C (DTC new brand) is not primary here -- they look at /services/sampling first.

## Primary Pains (Private-Label-Specific)

### pain_1: "Is private labeling a separate order or an add-on?"

- **Scenario**: ICP-A sourcing director already has an OEM PO in negotiation. She wants branded hangtags, care labels, and poly bags with her logo. Her last factory charged an extra $0.35/piece surcharge and required a separate 500-piece label MOQ on top of the garment MOQ. She visits /services/private-label to confirm this factory bundles it.
- **Consequence of not answering**: Buyer emails "How much extra for private labeling?" -- adding 1-2 days. Worse, she assumes extra cost and skips to a competitor listing "private label included."
- **Evidence**: The page must state "add-on to OEM/ODM, no extra MOQ, included in production price."

### pain_2: "What artwork files do I need to prepare?"

- **Scenario**: ICP-B gym owner has a logo in PNG only. He does not know whether the factory needs vector AI/PDF, what bleed to add, or whether colors must be Pantone-matched. He visits this page to get a checklist he can forward to his freelance designer.
- **Consequence of not answering**: Factory receives wrong-format files, 2-3 revision rounds on label proofs, delaying the order 5-7 days.
- **Evidence**: Artwork requirements section with file formats, bleed specs, color mode, and per-label-type size specs.

### pain_3: "What exactly is included in 'private label'?"

- **Scenario**: ICP-A has worked with factories that define "private label" differently -- some only do hangtags, others include care labels but not packaging. She needs a clear 5-component breakdown so she knows exactly what she gets.
- **Consequence of not answering**: Misaligned expectations surface at pre-shipment inspection when polybags arrive unbranded.
- **Evidence**: Explicit 5-component list: hangtag, care label, size label, polybag with logo, master carton with branding.

## Section Blueprint (6 sections)

### S1. Hero [full-bleed hero] -- 50vh, dark bg
- Eyebrow: "PRIVATE LABEL SERVICE"
- H1: "Private Label Activewear -- Your Brand, Our Production"
- Sub: You supply the artwork. We handle hangtags, care labels, size labels, branded polybags, and carton marking -- bundled into your OEM or ODM order at no extra MOQ.
- No CTA buttons in hero -- content below is short enough to scroll through.

### S2. What Private Label Includes [standard] -- ~100vh, white bg
- H2: "Five Label Components, One Production Run"
- Sub: Every private label order covers these five brand touchpoints -- all assembled during your existing OEM or ODM production.
- 5 cards in a row (desktop) / stacked (mobile):
  1. **Hangtag** -- Branded swing tag attached to each garment. Your logo, tagline, and product info on coated card stock.
  2. **Care Label** -- Woven or printed fabric label sewn at the neck or side seam. Washing instructions, fiber content, country of origin per your target market regs.
  3. **Size Label** -- Heat-transfer or woven size tab at the center back neck or waistband. Your size system (US/UK/EU) with your brand mark.
  4. **Polybag with Logo** -- Individual poly wrap printed with your logo and SKU barcode. Ready for FBA, 3PL, or retail shelf.
  5. **Master Carton Branding** -- Outer shipping carton with your logo, PO number, and packing list window. Clean unboxing for your warehouse team.
- OWNS: the 5-component breakdown (home only mentions "Private Label" in 1 service card)

### S3. Label Production Process [standard] -- auto height, tint bg #FAF7F2
- H2: "From Artwork to Attached -- 4 Steps"
- 4-step horizontal strip (desktop), vertical (mobile):
  1. **Receive Artwork** -- You send vector files (AI or PDF). We confirm Pantone colors, bleed, and label dimensions within 24 hours.
  2. **Print Labels** -- Hangtags printed on 350gsm coated stock. Care labels woven or printed on satin. Size labels heat-transfer or woven per your spec.
  3. **Assemble Kit** -- All 5 components grouped per SKU and staged at the packing station before garment production completes.
  4. **Attach During Packing** -- Labels attached, garments folded into branded polybags, packed into branded cartons. Ships as one finished order.

### S4. Artwork Requirements [standard] -- auto height, white bg
- H2: "Artwork Checklist"
- Sub: Forward this to your designer. Correct files on the first submission save 5-7 days on label proofs.
- 4 requirement rows:
  - **File Format**: Adobe Illustrator (.ai) or PDF with fonts outlined. No PNG/JPG -- vector only.
  - **Bleed**: 3mm bleed on all sides for hangtags. Care labels and size labels: no bleed needed (cut-to-edge by weave).
  - **Color Mode**: Pantone (PMS) for exact color matching across label types. CMYK accepted for hangtag print only.
  - **Size Specs**: Hangtag: 90 x 50mm standard (custom sizes accepted). Care label: 40 x 60mm. Size label: 20 x 40mm.

### S5. Order Specs [data-wall] -- compact, white bg with subtle border-top
- 4 stats in a row:
  - **"Add-on"** No extra MOQ -- private labeling bundles into your OEM or ODM order
  - **"Included"** In your OEM/ODM unit price -- no separate label surcharge
  - **"5"** Label components per garment (hangtag, care, size, polybag, carton)
  - **"Vector"** AI or PDF artwork required -- Pantone color matching standard

### S6. CTA [mid-cta] -- 30vh, dark bg
- H2: "Add Private Labeling to Your Order"
- Sub: "Send your artwork files with your next OEM or ODM inquiry. We confirm label specs within 24 hours."
- CTA button: "Send Your Artwork" -> /contact

- **No break-bands**: Page is under 6 content-heavy sections. Adding a break would pad a lean page unnecessarily. The tint background on S3 provides sufficient visual breathing.
