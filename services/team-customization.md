## S2 · What Gets Personalized (auto, white)

**H2:** Four Elements, One Roster File

**Sub:** Each piece leaves the line with its own combination of number, name, crest, and sponsor placement — matched to your CSV row by row.

### Player Number (Front + Back)
Full-panel sublimation, 0-99 range, front chest 8 cm and rear 28 cm height. Number font, outline weight, and shadow style locked per order for visual consistency across the squad.

### Player Name (Back, Above Number)
Uppercase block or custom typeface, max 14 characters. Kerning and arc curvature set once in the master template — the CSV populates each piece automatically.

### Team Crest (Left Chest)
Vector file required (AI/EPS/SVG). Positioned 8 cm below shoulder seam, max 10 cm diameter. Woven patch, sublimation print, or silicone badge — specified at order level, applied identically to every piece.

### Sponsor Block (Placement Varies)
Front center, upper back, or sleeve — placement confirmed during sample approval. Sponsor artwork supplied as a separate layer; the factory composites it onto the master template before the per-piece number/name merge.

## S3 · Roster CSV Workflow (auto, tint #FAF7F2)

**H2:** From Spreadsheet to Finished Kit in Four Steps

### Step 1 — Upload CSV
You fill three columns: player name, number, size. One row per piece. Save as .csv and attach to your inquiry.

### Step 2 — Data Validation
Our pre-press team checks for duplicate numbers, character-length overflows, and missing sizes within 24 hours. You receive a marked-up proof sheet if corrections are needed.

### Step 3 — Per-Piece Sublimation
Each piece runs through the sublimation line with its own merged file — number, name, crest, and sponsor block composited from your validated roster.

### Step 4 — QC Cross-Check
Every finished piece is scanned against the original roster row: correct name spelling, correct number, correct size label. Mismatches trigger reprint before packing.
