SPEC BEFORE QUOTE

A production tech-pack removes the guesswork before MOQ and price are discussed.

Sketches and reference photos are useful, but they do not tell the factory how to grade, stitch, trim or inspect the garment. This service converts the idea into a buyer-reviewable file: flat sketch, POM table, grading rule, construction notes and material/trim list.

  • Reference sample or photos are translated into measurable POM points and tolerance notes.
  • Pattern questions are resolved before sample cutting, including rise, armhole, neckline, inseam and compression target.
  • The output is designed to move directly into sampling, quotation or OEM bulk production.

Fit decisions

Compression level, strap setup, neckline depth and support panel notes are clarified before sampling.

Material list

Fabric base, GSM target, stretch requirement and trim choices are written into the spec.

POM table

Measurement points are defined in a way QC teams can repeat during sample and bulk inspection.

DELIVERABLES

Four Outputs in Every Tech-Pack

One PDF + DXF bundle — open it and start cutting.

Complete Tech-Pack

Flat sketch (front/back/detail), measurement chart, construction notes per seam, print placement coordinates.

Graded Pattern Set (XS-5XL)

Base-size pattern graded with activewear-specific rules. Output as paper patterns or DXF for Gerber, Lectra, Optitex.

Marker Layout

Fabric utilization map for your roll width. Target: 85%+ material yield.

Bill of Materials

Fabric, thread, elastic, hardware, care label, hangtag, polybag — all with supplier-neutral references.

PROCESS

From Reference to Production-Ready Spec

1

Send Your Reference

2

Pattern Team Builds Tech-Pack

One of 16 pattern-makers drafts the flat sketch, grades XS-5XL, creates the marker, compiles the BOM. 5-10 working days.
