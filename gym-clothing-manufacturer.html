Buyer Questions

Gym Clothing Manufacturer FAQ

Ten questions gym buyers ask before sending a tech-pack. Four for chain operators, four for established brands, two for first-time founders.

Multi-role gym chain sourcing
Can you really handle staff + trainer + retail + class apparel in one PO?

Yes. Our 12 production lines accommodate woven front-desk polos, heavy-jersey trainer wear, lifestyle knits for member retail, and performance fabric for class-specific apparel on parallel lines. One project manager owns the entire program. One Pantone reference. One label spec. One consolidated shipment.

What’s the MOQ if I split across 4 roles?

MOQ is 300/SKU per role, but the 300 splits across XS-XXL (not multiplied per size). A realistic chain PO is roughly: 60 front-desk polos · 200 trainer tees · 300 retail hoodies · 80 class-specific tanks = 4 SKUs, 640 total pieces. Lower per-role MOQ (60-100/SKU) available via Private Label combined with Gym Retail Pack.

How do you keep my logo identical across reorders and across the 4 roles?

Per-PO logo dimension QC at ±2mm tolerance, ΔE ≤ 1.5 vs your reference Pantone (published commitment). Single embroidery and screen-print supplier across all 4 roles. Cross-PO sample retained and matched against new PO at first-piece inspection.

Can I order the gym retail pack as a sample bundle before committing?

Yes. We send 4 sample SKUs (one per role) at USD 45 each (USD 180 total, waived after first PO). Sample-pack lead time 7-10 working days. You can pull photography from the sample pack before scheduling your bulk PO.

Brand-side gym apparel sourcing
What’s your abrasion-test threshold for trainer-grade gym wear?

Martindale ISO 12947-2 at 30,000+ cycles on all gym-tier fabric. Industry baseline for general activewear is 15-20K. We test every fabric lot before cut. Test report included in pre-shipment QC package on request.

How do you handle sweat-pH dye degradation on dark colors?

ISO 105-E04 acid sweat (pH 5.5) and alkaline sweat (pH 8.0) colorfastness at grade 4+ wet rub and dry rub. Dye system selected for oxidative stability against deodorant aluminum salts. Underarm yellowing on whites tested via AATCC 15 perspiration colorfastness.

Which fabric mills do you source from for gym apparel?

Eclat Textile (4-way stretch specialist), Formosa Taffeta (recycled-poly performance), Brookwood Performance (heavy-jersey knit specialist). All three OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified at mill level. Higg-traceable on request.

Can you produce GRS-certified recycled polyester gym apparel for eco lines?

Yes. GRS certification on file (CU 1014387 GRS-2024). Recycled polyester gym tees, joggers, and hoodies available with full chain-of-custody documentation. Used by 2 of our current customers for their eco capsules.

First-time gym sourcing
I don’t have a tech-pack. Can you still help with gym wear?

Yes — go with ODM. We have 50+ gym-cut block patterns covering training tees, joggers, 5″/7″/9″ inseam shorts, hoodies, and compression. You customize color, print, fabric tier, and labels. No tech-pack required.

Can I order just 1 sample to test before committing to 300?

Yes. 1-piece sample, USD 45 fee, produced in 5 working days when the fabric is on stock. Sample fee waived after first bulk PO. Samples available across all 6 gym subcategories before scaling.
