Privacy Policy

How Berun Active Wear collects, uses, shares, and protects the information you submit through our website and inquiry process.

Effective date: May 29, 2026

  1. Home
  2. /
  3. Privacy Policy

1.Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Berun Active Wear ("we", "us", "our") handles information collected through this website (berunactivewear.com) and through our business inquiry and quotation process. By using this site or submitting an inquiry, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the site or submit information to us.

2.Information We Collect

We collect two kinds of information:

  • Information you provide. Your name, company, business email, phone number, and country, along with the details of your project — including tech-packs, sketches, reference images, fabric preferences, and target quantities — submitted through our contact form, by email, or by messaging.
  • Information collected automatically. Your IP address, browser type, device, pages viewed, and referring source, gathered through cookies and standard web analytics.

3.How We Use Your Information

We use the information to respond to inquiries and prepare quotations, produce samples and fulfill orders, communicate with you about your project, operate and improve the website, send relevant business updates where you have opted in, and meet our legal and accounting obligations.

4.Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies that keep the site working and analytics cookies that help us understand how visitors use the site. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not work properly if you do.

5.How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with: service providers who run the website, communications, and analytics on our behalf; logistics and payment partners needed to fulfill an order; and authorities where disclosure is required by law. These partners are permitted to use your data only for the purpose we agree with them.

6.International Data Transfers

As a global manufacturer, we may process and store your information in countries other than the one where you are located. Where we do, we apply appropriate safeguards consistent with this policy and applicable law.

7.Data Retention

We keep inquiry and order information only for as long as needed to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected and to meet legal and accounting requirements. After that, we delete or anonymize it.

8.Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures — including access controls and encryption in transit — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9.Your Rights

Depending on where you live — including under the GDPR and the CCPA — you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, object to or restrict certain processing, and opt out of marketing communications. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

10.Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites. Their privacy practices are their own and are not covered by this policy; we encourage you to review their policies before sharing information.

11.Children's Privacy

This is a business-to-business website and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

12.Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top of the page reflects the latest version, and any material change will be posted on this page.

13.Contact Us

For any question or request about this policy or your data, email us at sales@berunactivewear.com.
