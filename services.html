Production Services

Activewear Manufacturing Services — From Tech-Pack to Delivery

Seven production tracks for brands at every stage. Whether you bring a finished tech-pack, a sketch, or a sample you want repeated — there is a track that starts where you are.

ROUTE THE RFQ

Choose the service path by what your buyer file already contains.

The fastest RFQ is not always the shortest form. It is the one routed to the right production lane: finished tech-pack, base-pattern launch, sample-only review, label kit, artwork file, roster CSV or full pattern development.

  • OEM is for buyers with a finished spec and clear production target.
  • ODM is for launch programs that can start from a stable factory base pattern.
  • Print, label and team customization are add-on workflows that need their own approval records.
