**Sub**: ""
**内容**:

5-step horizontal process strip:

1. **Inquiry** — You send your requirements or tech-pack. We confirm service track, MOQ, and timeline within 24 hours. (Day 0)
2. **Sample Approval** — We produce 1-3 samples for fit, color, and construction sign-off. (5-12 days)

5. **Shipment** — Pre-shipment report shared. Goods released to your freight forwarder or our logistics partner. (Day 35-42)
