## 板块 2: Legal Prose [standard]
**H2**: (none — long-form legal body with subheadings)
**内容**:
1. **Introduction** — Scope: this site (berunactivewear.com) and our B2B inquiry/quotation process. By using the site or submitting an inquiry you agree to this policy.
2. **Information We Collect** —
   - *Information you provide*: name, company, business email, phone, country, and the details of your project (tech-packs, sketches, reference images, target quantities) submitted through our contact form or by email/messaging.
   - *Information collected automatically*: IP address, browser type, device, pages viewed, and referring source, via cookies and standard web analytics.
3. **How We Use Your Information** — respond to inquiries and prepare quotations; produce samples and fulfill orders; communicate about your project and account; improve the website and our services; send relevant business updates where you have opted in; meet legal and compliance obligations.
4. **Cookies & Tracking** — essential cookies for site function plus analytics cookies to understand traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some may affect site features.
5. **How We Share Information** — we do **not** sell your personal data. We share it only with: service providers who operate the site, communications, and analytics on our behalf; logistics and payment partners needed to fulfill an order; and authorities where required by law. Partners are bound to use the data only for the agreed purpose.
6. **International Data Transfers** — as a global manufacturer we may process and store data in countries other than yours. We apply appropriate safeguards consistent with this policy.
7. **Data Retention** — we keep inquiry and order data only as long as needed for the purpose collected and for legal/accounting requirements, then delete or anonymize it.
8. **Data Security** — we use reasonable technical and organizational measures (access controls, encryption in transit) to protect your information. No method of transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
9. **Your Rights** — depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR and CCPA), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, object to certain processing, or opt out of marketing. Contact us to exercise these rights.
10. **Third-Party Links** — our site may link to third-party sites; their privacy practices are their own and not covered by this policy.
11. **Children's Privacy** — this is a B2B website not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.
12. **Changes to This Policy** — we may update this policy; the "Effective date" above reflects the latest version, and material changes will be posted on this page.
13. **Contact Us** — questions or requests: email sales@berunactivewear.com.
