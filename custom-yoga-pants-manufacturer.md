## 板块 8: Leggings Manufacturing FAQ [accordion]
**H2**: Custom Leggings Manufacturing — Questions Buyers Ask
**Sub**: The minimums, sampling, grading, and reorder answers that decide whether we are your factory.
**内容**: 8-item accordion (4 sourcing-director + 4 founder), 50–80 words each:
1. **Are flare and wide-leg leggings made in-house?** Yes. As a flare leggings manufacturer we cut all eight fits on our own floor — flare and wide-leg on dedicated pattern grading, seamless on a circular-knit line. Your whole range runs through one dye batch, so the black on your flare matches the black on your compression legging. No subcontracting, no cross-supplier color drift.
2. **What does MOQ 300 mean — per size or total?** Total. MOQ is 300 pieces per style, size-split across XS–3XL as you choose (for example 60 each across five sizes), not 300 per size. That keeps a first run realistic for a new fit without forcing 1,500 pieces.
3. **How fast is a sample, and what does it cost?** A sample is 1 piece at USD 45, ready in 5 working days when the fabric is in stock, or 12 working days for to-order fabric. You approve fit, fabric, and color on the sample before any bulk is cut.
4. **Will my plus sizes fit like the sample?** Yes. We grade 1X–3X on a separate plus block with proportional rise, gusset, and waistband-to-hip grading — not a stretched medium. The waistband holds and the crotch sits right at 2X and 3X, where linear grading usually fails.
5. **How consistent is color across reorders?** We hold Pantone color to ΔE ≤1.5 and read a spectrophotometer value per fabric lot in your pre-shipment package. Reorder black matches first-order black, so your line stays consistent season to season.
6. **What is the private label minimum?** Private label runs on our existing leggings blanks at a lower entry than full OEM, with your woven label, hangtag, and packaging. It is the fastest route to a first PO; send us your label kit and target fits to confirm.
7. **What fabrics and weights can I choose?** GSM runs 230–320 across the six fits — nylon-spandex and Lycra T-400 for compression, modal blends for relaxed, circular-knit for seamless. Recycled GRS yarn and brushed finishes are available, all certified to OEKO-TEX Standard 100 #23.HCN.74521.
8. **How fast are reorders once a style is approved?** Once a style is approved and the pattern is on file, bulk reorders run 35–42 days. Color and fit are locked to your approved sample, so reorders skip re-sampling and ship against the same spec.
